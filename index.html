Frequently asked questions

What data do you have?

We have multiple data points of last-mile delivery companies: key contact info (emails, phone numbers and websites), physical addresses, mailing address, company owner names (with their roles, contacts such as emails, phone numbers, LinkedIn accounts), assigned warehouse locations, fleet size records, driver count records, insurance records and contractor type information.

How often is your data updated?

We update our database on a daily basis, with verification of key contact information. Our team continuously monitors contractor registrations, DOT filings, and business registrations to ensure accuracy.

What sources do you use?

We combine public records (DOT, SOS filings), proprietary research, industry partnerships, and direct verification. Our methodology includes cross-referencing multiple sources to ensure accuracy.

Can I export the data?

Yes, all plans include CSV and Microsoft Excel export capabilities.
